MAMA BIDII CHAMA CONSTITUTION

ARTICLE 1: MEMBERSHIP & CONTRIBUTIONS
Section 1.1: Monthly contribution is KES 2,000 due by 5th of each month
Section 1.2: Late fee of KES 200 per week applies after due date
Section 1.3: Three consecutive missed contributions result in automatic suspension
Section 1.4: Suspended members must clear all arrears to be reinstated

ARTICLE 2: LOANS
Section 2.1: Members eligible after 6 months of consistent contributions
Section 2.2: Maximum loan is 3 times total contributions
Section 2.3: Interest rate is 10% per annum
Section 2.4: Repayment period maximum 12 months
Section 2.5: No new loans while existing loan is outstanding

ARTICLE 3: WITHDRAWAL
Section 3.1: Member must give 30 days written notice
Section 3.2: Member entitled only to contributions made (no interest)
Section 3.3: Outstanding loans must be cleared before withdrawal
Section 3.4: No withdrawal during loan repayment period

ARTICLE 4: EMERGENCY FUND
Section 4.1: 10% of monthly contributions go to emergency fund
Section 4.2: Only accessible for medical emergencies
Section 4.3: Supporting documents required (hospital receipt, doctor's note)
Section 4.4: Committee approval needed within 48 hours

ARTICLE 5: DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Section 5.1: All disputes referred to arbitration
Section 5.2: Treasurer must present financial records
Section 5.3: Decision made within 14 days
Section 5.4: Majority vote required for final resolution
